description= This mod replaces the vanilla helicopter event with a more dynamic version which is both challenging and fair. Furthermore, the mod was designed to be easily expandable and modifiable in itself.*
description= 
description= [h3]To make suggestions, bug reports, contribute visit the [url=https://github.com/sharkster91/ExpandedHelicopterEvents]Github here[/url].[/h3]
description= 
description= [h2]Features:[/h2]
description= [list]
description= [*] Events become less frequent and end at around a month. (Note this factors in the "months after apocalypse" sandbox setting.)
description= 
description= [*] Helicopters (and other flying events) will not fly in extremely bad weather, and have a relative chance to crash based on how less than ideal the weather is.
description= 
description= [*] Crashed helicopters are vehicle objects, which can be looted and slept in.
description= 
description= [*] Multiple types of helicopters or other flying events.
description= 
description= [*] Players can actually hide from / avoid the helicopter.
description= 
description= [*] The helicopter casts a shadow to indicate where it is in relation to the player.
description= 
description= [*] Helicopters can fire on targets. This does not instantly kill but does gravely wound targets and shred clothing.
description= 
description= [*] Fully voiced announcements, which can be easily expanded upon by others in the future.
description= 
description= [*] MP/CO-OP: When selecting targets helicopters will prefer players not in forested areas.
description= 
description= [*] Helicopters can drop items or care packages for the player.
description= [/list]
description= 
description= [h2]Events Currently in the mod:[/h2]
description= *Helicopter events are written so that their features can be mixed and matched for different "types" of helicopters.
description= 
description= [list]
description= [*] (Military) Increasingly Hostile Helicopter: Initially this helicopter will only make announcements but will over time begin firing on targets; at first towards the undead then later, towards players as well.
description= 
description= [*] Police Helicopter: These helicopters will patrol early on and fire on undead right away.
description= 
description= [*] News Helicopter: These helicopters will follow their target and most closely resemble the vanilla event. However, the player can use cover as well as the indoors to flee the area.
description= 
description= [*] Jets: While not strictly a "helicopter" the jet events whizz near by the player to keep things interesting and move horde of undead early on.
description= [/list]

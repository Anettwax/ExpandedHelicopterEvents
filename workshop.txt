description=[h2]FAQ:[/h2]
description=What version of zomboid is this for?
description=[b]Build 41+[/b]
description=
description=Does this mod with with non-vanilla maps?
description=[b]Yes. Any map, any size.[/b]
description=
description=Does this work with on-going/old saves?
description=[b]Applying the mod to an ongoing save will use default settings for events. Events will taper off becoming less frequent up to 30 days. For more options see the sandbox settings when you create a save.[/b]
description=
description= [h2]Features:[/h2]
description= [list]
description= [*] Events become less frequent and end at around a month. (Note this factors in the "months after apocalypse" sandbox setting.)
description=
description= [*] Helicopters (and other flying events) will not fly in extremely bad weather, and have a relative chance to crash based on how less than ideal the weather is.
description=
description= [*] Crashed helicopters are vehicle objects, which can be looted and slept in.
description=
description= [*] Multiple types of helicopters or other flying events.
description=
description= [*] Players can actually hide from / avoid the helicopter.
description=
description= [*] The helicopter casts a shadow to indicate where it is in relation to the player.
description=
description= [*] Helicopters can fire on targets. This does not instantly kill but does gravely wound targets and shred clothing.
description=
description= [*] Fully voiced announcements, which can be easily expanded upon by others in the future.
description=
description= [*] CO-OP/NPC/Eventual MP Support: The helicopters will target people depending on their location; prefer targets in the cities over those in the forest.
description=
description= [*] Helicopters can drop items or care packages for the player.
description=
description= [*] Helicopter events are registered on AEBS (radio system) just like vanilla events!
description= [/list]
description=
description= [h2]Events Currently in the mod:[/h2]
description= *Helicopter events are written so that their features can be mixed and matched for different "types" of helicopters.
description=
description= [list]
description= [h2]EARLY GAME:[/h2]
description= [*] (Military) Increasingly Hostile Helicopter: Initially this helicopter will only make announcements but will over time begin firing on targets; at first towards the undead then later, towards players as well.
description=
description= [*] Police Helicopter: These helicopters will patrol early on and fire on undead right away.
description=
description= [*] News Helicopter: These helicopters will follow their target and most closely resemble the vanilla event. However, the player can use cover as well as the indoors to flee the area.
description=
description= [*] Passing Jets: While not strictly a "helicopter" the jet events whizz near by the player to keep things interesting and move horde of undead early on.
description= 
description= [*] Jet bombings: As the game world progresses, jets will begin bombing targets offscreen.
description= 
description= [*] Air raid siren: An air raid siren will wail as the game progresses to signal incoming hostile events.
description= 
description= [*] FEMA Emergency Drop: On occasion and before civilization collapses, a helicopter will drop off an emergency supply drop.
description= 
description= [h2]LATE GAME:[/h2]
description= [*] The Wolverines: A group of raiders may fly into the area late in the game. Their actions range can range from innocuous to incredibly adverse.
description= 
description= [*] Survivors: A group of survivors may fly around the area looking for supplies and will buzz past you.
description= 
description= [*] Plane Air Drop: Deep into the apocalypse, you may encounter a mysterious aircraft that will drop a crate of supplies.
description= [/list]
